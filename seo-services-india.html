Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our SEO services in India

SEO services in India typically cost between ₹10,000 and ₹60,000 per month depending on the competitiveness of your industry, the number of keywords targeted, and the scope of optimization. Professional SEO agencies provide services such as keyword research, technical SEO, content optimization, link building, and ongoing performance tracking to improve rankings and organic traffic.

SEO usually takes around 3 to 6 months to start showing noticeable improvements in rankings and traffic. The timeline depends on factors such as competition, website authority, content quality, and backlink profile. Consistent optimization helps businesses achieve long-term visibility and sustainable growth in search engines.

SEO and Google Ads serve different purposes. SEO focuses on long-term organic traffic and building authority in search results, while Google Ads delivers immediate visibility and faster lead generation. Many successful businesses use both strategies together to maximize online growth and reach potential customers at different stages of the buying journey.

To choose the right SEO agency in India, look for proven experience, transparent reporting, ethical SEO practices, and a strong portfolio of results. A reliable agency will conduct detailed keyword research, provide technical optimization, build high-quality backlinks, and regularly report performance metrics such as rankings, traffic, and conversions.
