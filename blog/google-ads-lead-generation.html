Common SEO Mistakes Meerut Businesses Make

Many businesses waste time and money on ineffective SEO tactics. Here are mistakes to avoid:

  • Keyword Stuffing: Overusing keywords unnaturally in content, which Google penalizes
  • Ignoring Mobile Optimization: With 70%+ searches happening on mobile, a non-mobile-friendly site kills your rankings
  • Buying Backlinks: Purchasing links from low-quality sites results in Google penalties
  • Duplicate Content: Copying content from other websites damages your credibility and rankings
  • Neglecting Technical SEO: Slow loading speeds, broken links, and poor site structure hurt rankings
  • No Local SEO Strategy: Missing out on local customers who are ready to buy
